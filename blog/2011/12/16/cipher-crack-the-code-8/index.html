Click the button:

button.htm:

Yes, it’s a button. It knows the answers of the universe. It knows the meaning of life. It knows where you live. Actually, it doesn’t, because it’s just a button.

So stop reading this sarcastic crap and get back to the bloody level.

According to this, this paragraph is suggesting 42.

F1 normally means “Help”, so help.htm:

Nope, I’m afraid I can’t give you any help. Fresh out. Go get your own.

F1 could also imply formula1, so formula1.htm:

Vrooooooooom….you just zoomed waaay off track.

No clue now. I tried several methods to change URL, it just simply won’t work. What can a number mean for a clueless level? Maybe like previous levels for threshold? But whate ver I tried, no luck.

Help and Button, wait a second, does it literally mean the help button of the first page of the game? I picked up the picture and fill light by Picasa, oops, there are 2 numbers in either the top right corner or the bottom right corner:

3, 7

And then I examine the EXIF information,

title: 5
subject: 5
tags: 1;4

Several trials, finally it means 5 subjects of number:

3,7,5,1,4

And according to help page, the initial letters of description of the matching levels are:

Level 3: Ever
Level 7: You
Level 5: What
Level 1: Creepy
Level 4: Hmm

After anagram calculation, the answer is chewy.
