Neverwinter Nights on Parallels Desktop 8

Setup

Once upon a time, I was an adventure in the realm of Neversummer, my soul binded to The Circle, as Manyface Master. Dragons were my enemies but I could turn to dragon shape and maintain the balance of nature.
Ward Di’makiir

It’s very exciting that I can connect to Neversummer again. It’s even happier that my account is still accessible. Though my shifter build turns into Druid/Rogue/Blackguard Build.

At the very beginning, I am looking for NWN mac version, but unfortunately, Apple drop Rosseta Since Lion, which means PowerPC executable NWN cannot run on OS X. And my Chinese version of NWN cannot upgrade to 1.69. The upgrade applications only works on English version and the publisher of Chinese version already bankcrupted long time ago. Luckily, the CD-KEY is still valid, I just need to find a English copy. To accompany with Mac NWN, Snow Leopard Server installation in Parallels Desktop is required. More troublesome than Windows version.

So at last, I just install an Windows XP in my PD8. and then Official Campaign and 2 Expansion Packs: SoU and HoU. It’s just work, with the help of parallels desktop tools installed, I can even play the game through Splashtop remotely to the virtual machine of my iMac. performance is not an issue. The game runs smoothly with the best quality of texture and 2560x1440 resolution.

Shifter Build notes

As I mentioned, my experience is outdated, the server already turns into 4.0 while at my time was 3.5. I appreciate the philosophy of balance a lot, so this time I still choose The Circle as my first build of Shiter.

Rufio had an detailed description in nsrealm forum.. The main points is:

  • Due to the BAB progression of Druid and Shifter are both 3 per 4 levels, the build of Shifter and Druid should be round up to multiples of 4.
  • Spot Skill is very important for characters.

I also take some notes from wiki in NSRealm, including pages of Druid, Shifter, Shifting guide.

  • Unlike Official Campaign, in Neversummer, Magical Bonuses from all items except weapons merge into all forms. Weapon bonus only merge into forms that use melee weapons. It means all shape other than dragons can have the benefits of extra damage.
