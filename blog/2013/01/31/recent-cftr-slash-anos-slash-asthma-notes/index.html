Recent CFTR/ANOs/asthma notes

Nedd4-2 does not ubiquitinate and thereby regulate wt-CFTR in human airway epithelial cells.loss of NEDD4-2 in adult renal tubules causes a new form of mild, salt-sensitive hypertension without hyperkalemia that is characterized by upregulation of NCC, elevation of β/γENaC, but not αENaC, and a normal Na+/K+ balance maintained by downregulation of ENaC activity and upregulation of ROMK. NEDD8 (neural precursor cell expressed, developmentally down-regulated 8) is a ubiquitin-like molecule whose action on modifying protein substrates is critical in various cellular functions. This study thus identifies an important role for protein neddylation in T-cell function, which may serve as a therapeutic target for inflammatory diseases.The E3 ubiquitin ligase midline 1 promotes allergen and rhinovirus-induced asthma by inhibiting protein phosphatase 2A activity. including the expression of interleukin-25 (IL-25), IL-33 and CCL20, IL-5 and IL-13 release, nuclear factor (NF)κB activity, p38 mitogen-activated protein kinase (MAPK) phosphorylation, accumulation of eosinophils, T lymphocytes and myeloid dendritic cells, and the number of mucus-producing cells.

Chloroplast biogenesis is regulated by direct action of the ubiquitin-proteasome system.

A review in Respiratory summarizes that the genes associated with HAPE include those in the renin-angiotensin-aldosterone system pathway, the nitric oxide pathway and the hypoxia-inducible factor pathway. Other genes associated with HAPE include tyrosine hydroxylase (TH), vascular endothelial growth factor (VEGF), pulmonary surfactant proteins and β(2)-adrenergic receptor. Among the genes evaluated, polymorphisms of NOS3, ACE, CYP11B2, Hsp70 and endothelin-1 and pulmonary surfactant proteins A1 and A2 were shown to be associated with HAPE incidence, while associations between TH, VEGF and HAPE remain to be fully elucidated. In young population, pulmonary artery pressure incease to 2 folds at 3500m to low altitude.. TRPV1 in Aδ neurons, which are mechanosensitive neurons, may be upregulated by allergen sensitization.

Eps8, a bi-functional actin cytoskeleton remodeller, is required for proper mitotic progression and regulated by ubiquitinization.
