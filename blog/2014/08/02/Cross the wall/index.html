Cross the wall

The notorious Great Fire Wall is always the nightmare for travellers to mainland. There are serveral methods to overcome the blocking. Basically, they are static proxy, dynamic proxy, and VPN. 3 years ago, I had set up php-proxy in my institute server, but strangely, it’s not working now.

Recently a friend in mainland need VPN to get access to web service such as facebook or youtube. Potentially I may need it as well. So, to help her and prepare a plan B to myself in case I paid a visit, here is my review.

There are actually quite a number of different specifications for VPN. IMHO, actually it is not a very important issue for entertainment or home users: we did not care the safety issues to the extent of business users. Of cause, it may have some minor difference on setting, but all 3 modes of VPN get plenty of tutorials online.( PPTP ).

A friend recommended shadowshock, it seems like a dynamic VPN service, may not be very reliable. For other free of charge alternatives, like GoBrowse may share the same weakness, limited speed and stability.

Previously, Onavo Protect shipped a VPN app on iOS, but now it’s blocked. But after that, VPN Gate and Open VPN is still available.

There is a discussion on many VPN service in x-berry. For example, OpenVPN is quite popular and there are many online tutorials on the web. (1, 2, 3 ). There are many others like iVPN, pureVPN and Astrill VPN, which recently published their iOS app in US store. Also , a recent VPN service called VPN Tech Runo gains the reputation of cheap and user-friendly for Chinese people.
